About

i D E S I G N is a prestigious Design Academy dedicated to cultivating the creative potential of children by introducing them to the captivating world of design and its practical applications. We are committed to nurturing young minds, fostering their imagination, and equipping them with valuable skills that will serve as a foundation for their future endeavors.

Why Choose US?

  1. Comprehensive Design Education: At i D E S I G N, we offer a comprehensive design education tailored specifically for children. Our curriculum is designed to introduce them to various design disciplines, including graphic design, industrial design, fashion design, and more. Through our programs, students gain a well-rounded understanding of the design field.

  2. Experienced Instructors: Our team of highly experienced and passionate instructors are dedicated to inspiring and mentoring young designers. They bring a wealth of industry knowledge and expeise to the classroom, ensuring that each student receives the best guidance and support.

  3. Project-Based Learning: We firmly believe in the power of hands-on, project-based learning. Our teaching approach is centered around real-world design projects that engage students in practical problem-solving, creative thinking, and collaboration. This approach not only enhances their design skills but also prepares them for future challenges.
